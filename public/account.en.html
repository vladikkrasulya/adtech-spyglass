What's tracked, what isn't

Spyglass is zero-knowledge for payloads. The cabinet's "Activity" stats come from a small per-user log that records ONLY the metadata below. The bid request and bid response bodies are NEVER persisted to that log — they only enter the validator, findings come out, the bodies are discarded.

Tracked timestamp · oRTB version · status (clean/warnings/errors) · format (banner/video/native/...) · finding counts (errors / warnings / total)
Not tracked payload bodies · individual finding ids · IP · user-agent · partner identity · timestamps below day-resolution in aggregates
Anonymous calls not logged
Account deletion log is cascade-deleted (foreign key)

Encryption & recovery

Your library is encrypted with a key derived from your password. A recovery key (32-char hex) was shown once at registration — keep it in a password manager. If you lose both, the library can only be wiped, not recovered.

Account actions

Sign out clears the session cookie and locks your library. To remove your account entirely, write to hi@kyivtech.com.ua — automated account deletion is intentionally not exposed yet (rare, irreversible, easy to misclick).
