The Spyglass blog is where we share knowledge about OpenRTB internals, dissect tricky spec edge cases, and publish curated news from the programmatic advertising world.

**What you'll find here:**

- **News** — handpicked items from the adtech firehose: spec updates, SSP/DSP announcements, IAB Tech Lab releases.
- **Deep-dives** — technical breakdowns of specific OpenRTB fields, common bid request/response errors, and how Spyglass detects them.
- **Guides** — step-by-step walkthroughs: from basic setup to integrating Spyglass into your QA pipeline.

Posts appear as both editorial (written by the team) and firehose-sourced — curated and reviewed before publication. The RSS feed is available at `/blog/rss.xml`.
